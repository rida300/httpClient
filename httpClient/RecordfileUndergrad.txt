         <h3>Computer Science Minor</h3>
<p><span>The Department of Computer Science offers a minor in computer science, targeted towards engineering students as an area of special interest outside their major. The minor focuses on mathematical and logical foundations for programming. The following courses must be completed:</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Program Requirements (16 credit hours)</h3>
<p>Prerequisites (in addition to any prerequisites for the required courses):</p>
<ul><li class="acalog-course"><a href="https://catalog.k-state.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=42&amp;poid=13853">MATH 220 - Analytic Geometry and Calculus I</a>&nbsp;<strong>Credits:</strong>&nbsp;4</li></ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Required courses</h3>
<ul><li class="acalog-course"><a href="https://catalog.k-state.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=42&amp;poid=13853">CIS 115 - Introduction to Computing Science</a>&nbsp;<strong>Credits:</strong>&nbsp;3</li><li class="acalog-course"><a href="https://catalog.k-state.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=42&amp;poid=13853">CIS 200 - Programming Fundamentals</a>&nbsp;<strong>Credits:</strong>&nbsp;4</li><li class="acalog-course"><a href="https://catalog.k-state.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=42&amp;poid=13853">CIS 300 - Data and Program Structures</a>&nbsp;<strong>Credits:</strong>&nbsp;3</li><li class="acalog-course"><a href="https://catalog.k-state.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=42&amp;poid=13853">CIS 301 - Logical Foundations of Programming</a>&nbsp;<strong>Credits:</strong>&nbsp;3</li><li class="acalog-course"><a href="https://catalog.k-state.edu/preview_program.php?catoid=42&amp;poid=13853">ECE 241 - Introduction to Computer Engineering</a>&nbsp;<strong>Credits:</strong>&nbsp;3</li></ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
